このアプリについて

Weather and Temperature Chart Quiz" is a simple geography quiz that visualizes climate data (temperature and precipitation) for random locations around the world and asks you to guess where that location is. It is a free browser game aimed at learning and cultivating a sense of climate.

使っている技術・データ

  • Maps：It is drawn using Leaflet, and the map tiles are derived from OpenStreetMap (OSM).
  • Climo Data（Climographs）：We use the Open-Meteo API to obtain and graph temperature and precipitation data in real time.

About Privacy

We do not collect location information or use user accounts. There are no functions to save game play history or personal information (rankings or login).

Notes

  • Open-Meteo data may be missing for some locations. In such cases, we will switch to another location.
  • In the event of heavy traffic, maps may not be displayed due to limitations on the map tile provider (in which case we plan to switch tile providers).
